Origins

 Monstera species are native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly in countries like Mexico, Panama, Costa Rica, Guatemala, and Brazil.

 In their natural habitat, Monstera plants climb trees using their aerial roots, which helps them reach sunlight while staying rooted in nutrient-rich, moist soil.
